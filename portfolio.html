    Discover-Devs | Django Web-App

    A platform that enables developers to showcase their work and get discovered by customers around the globe. Developers can sign up to create their profiles. They can add their skills and projects to their profile and launch it for everyone to see. They can rate and comment on each other's work. All users have access to a messaging app right under their profile. Users without a profile can send a message to any developer.

    This Web-App was deployed with ElasticBeanstalk, S3, CloudFront, Route 53, and uses s3 to store images & PostgreSQL to store user data.
